About Company

Anubhav India was established by Aakash, Alinda, Drisya, Elrica and Sikandar as a partnership firm. Our travel company gives authentic experience of India by exploring the unexplored. Anubhav India is an organization dedicated to development projects at the grass root level, in the sectors of agri- community tourism, revival of traditional art and culture, and education. Our Efforts are directed towards sustained economic growth and development while preserving the local heritage, ethos and tradition of the people and their surroundings. Our endeavours are varied. It involves consolidating and empowering rural women under one brand identity through the self-help group model; creating branded home stays; and the revival of endangered traditional art forms. We customize holidays that offer the perfect escape to unique destinations around India along with exclusive experiences, designed as per your choice and planned at your place.
